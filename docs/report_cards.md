# Report Cards

Autonomous runs can produce a report card with:

- autonomy score
- completeness score
- quality score
- reliability score
- safety score
- overall grade

The v2 card also records contract score, done-definition score, artifact quality, consistency, verification honesty, export score, and an `A+` through `F` grade.

Report cards are written with workspace artifacts and included in export packages when available. They are simple local quality signals, not proof of correctness.
